# 🧫 Anti-biofilm Activity of Onion Peel Extract against Bacillus subtilis

## 🔍 Overview

This project evaluates the potential of onion peel extract as a natural antibiofilm agent against *Bacillus subtilis*, focusing on its antimicrobial properties and biofilm inhibition capacity.

---

## ⚙️ Methodology

* Preparation of onion peel extract
* Phytochemical analysis (Total Phenolic Content, Total Flavonoid Content)
* Antibacterial activity using agar well diffusion method
* Biofilm quantification using crystal violet assay
* Microscopic observation of biofilm structure

---

## 📊 Key Results

* Zone of inhibition: ~12–13 mm
* Biofilm inhibition: up to ~80% at higher concentrations
* High phenolic and flavonoid content detected
* Significant disruption of biofilm formation observed

---

## 🧪 Techniques Used

* Microbial culture techniques
* Spectrophotometry
* Biofilm assay (96-well plate method)
* Light microscopy

---

## 🌱 Applications

* Development of eco-friendly antimicrobial agents
* Biofilm control in medical and industrial settings
* Potential use in food safety and water treatment
